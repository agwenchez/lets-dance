Our Development Journey



Dance Into Space Foundation works in the mainstream Contemporary dance arts & aims at providing quality training and life skills through dance arts for advocacy and awareness. The organisation also provides opportunities to young people with disabilities to discover their potentials together and Break Barriers of alienation and despair for their full integration into society through the integrated mixed able dance projects and other modes of artistic expression.
