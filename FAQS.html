FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQS)

-Dance Into Space exists to foster the appreciation, practice and training in performing arts especially dance and theatre for entertainment, education and in community development..

-Dance Into Space has trained and worked with approximately 300 artistes in seven counties and has continued to work closely with these groups fostering artistic and cultural exchanges through workshops and joint productions.
-Dance Into Space continues to integrate people with disability into performance arts while breaking the taboos and stigma associated with disability.
-Dance Into Space is a place where choreographies are developed by the company and being used as a tool for advocacy.

-Dance Into Space has trained close to 100 dancers with disability and also created new mixed ability groups in Kenya.
-Dance Into Space has also received the decentralization projects award from Arterial Network (S.A) and Africalia Belgium for DMR.
-Dance Into Space has decentralized contemporary dance by creating new groups, building new audiences and creating performance and training spaces in new areas.

-Do you have any story that you would wish to share through Contemporary dance that will help shape the society positively? Contact us and let us work together.

-Dance Into Space has worked and continued to work alongside people with and without disabilities through performance art to help educate the society and break the stigma, taboos and discrimination associated with disability. Through this we beleive we are on course to change the world through "art-ivsm" and positive activism.

By staying positive through advocating and creating awareness with performance art and ensuring that policy makers hear our voice and also believing that there is a brighter tomorrow.
